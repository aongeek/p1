Our Projects




Our strength is in our ability to execute projects safely, on time and on budget. We have built strong relationships with the major pipeline owners and operators within Canada. These relationships are established through our ability to meet project demands.

Stoney Creek Project

Our Stoney Creek Project with The Pipeline experts Inc. is located near Stoney Creek, The project involved the construction of 30 inch natural gas pipelines.
BCR H1

Our BCR H1 Unit project with Canadawide Pipelines Ltd. is located near Black creek river . This project involved the Removal and relocation of 15 pumping units.

uptown Project

Our uptown Project with Trillium Energy Inc. is located west of St. johns. This project involved the installation of 18 inch continuous assembly line system. The system consisted of a fresh water source, as well as, gas collection and gathering units.
